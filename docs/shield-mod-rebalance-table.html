Pokey's Shield Mod Rebalance — the mods

Three levers (capacity, recharge rate, recharge delay) become clear archetypes. Every value is fixed (no roll RNG) and there are no maluses. Capacity is the always-good tier scalar; the honest trade-off is physical — a bigger buffer refills slower. The defining effect is emphasized.

Reductions (−) are the good direction — a shorter recharge delay means the shield starts refilling sooner.  ·  The capacity trade-off: time-to-full ≈ delay + capacity ÷ rate, so Bastion tanks the hardest hit but recovers slowest; Regenerator recovers fastest; Targe splits the difference.  ·  X4: Foundations v9.0

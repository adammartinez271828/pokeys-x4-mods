Everything else is parked. The remaining engine mods (Aestus, Anchor, Crab, Creatine, Juno, Minuteman, Pusher, Reaver, Spinner, Spur, Twister at Basic; Mira, Propeller at Enhanced) are pinned to a token floor (+5% Basic, +10% Enhanced). The wares can't be removed from the game, so they're made harmless rather than the pick — the archetype carriers above are always the better craft.
Reductions (−) are the good direction — a shorter travel spool-up or lighter charge is faster.  ·  Forward thrust feeds boost + travel too, so the Interceptor's modest % is worth as much as a specialist's bigger single-axis number.  ·  X4: Foundations v9.0

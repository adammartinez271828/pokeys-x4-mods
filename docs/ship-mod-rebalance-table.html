Pokey's Hull Mod Rebalance — the mods

The most tangled equipment section — 11 stat families — resolved into four archetypes: Racer, Tank, Ghost, Explorer, one carrier per tier. Every value is fixed (no roll RNG). Max hull is the always-good tier sweetener, riding on every Enhanced (+10%) and Exceptional (+20%) mod. The defining effect is emphasized.

Everything else is parked. Six Basic wares fold into the archetypes above and are pinned to a token floor: Polisher (drag −3%), Scope (radar +10%), and the four single-store capacity mods — Barrage (+3 countermeasures), Rack (+3 deployables), Bandolier (+3 missiles), Garage (+3 units). Tank carries all four consumable stores at once, so the singles are never the pick.
Reductions (−) are the good direction — less mass means faster acceleration, less drag means higher top speed, a lower signature means shorter enemy detection range.  ·  Consumables = countermeasures, deployables (drones/satellites), missiles and units; the counts are flat additions.  ·  X4: Foundations v9.0
